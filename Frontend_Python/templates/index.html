About Our Product

All in one tool to identify whom to reach out

Direct marketing enables banks and other financial organizations to focus on those customers who present the likelihood of subscribing to their products, offers, and other packages. Most often than not, identifying these group of customers poses a challenge to financial institutions.

TDPredictor aims to increase campaign efficiency by identifying the main factors that affect the success of a campaign and predicting whether the campaign will be successful to a certain client, namely, whether the client will subscribe a term deposit. Hence, our product will enable the banks to arrange a better management of available resources by focusing on the potential customers “picked” by our tool, which will improve their efficiency a lot.

In line with the aforementioned, our tool considered the typical case of bank direct marketing campaign to predict customer response to bank direct marketing by applying five classifiers namely:

Decision Tree
Random Forest
Logistic Regression
KNN
